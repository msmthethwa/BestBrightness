Welcome to the Store Management System

This system offers a comprehensive set of tools to help you manage every aspect of your store. Use the menu above to navigate through the system's features:

  • Manage Employees: Accept, update, view, and delete employee records.
  • Manage Customers: Accept, view, update, and delete customer records.
  • Assign Privileges: Assign relevant privileges to all system users.
  • Stock Requests: Create, send, update, or cancel stock requests with records of changes.
  • Receive Stock: Receive and record stock from the warehouse, generating and saving stock receipt slips.
  • Financial Reports: Generate monthly and annual financial reports.
  • Product Information: Record and update product information for timely access by online customers.
  • Restricted Access: Restrict system access to registered and authorized users only.

The Store Management System is designed to be user-friendly and efficient.
